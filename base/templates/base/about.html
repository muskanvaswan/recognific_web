About Us

Recognific is a user-friendly, web-based attendance model that makes the use of facial recognition. To record their attendance ,a student can look into the camera and is recognized by the model based on the photos given by the teacher while initializing the class. When a match is confirmed the time and the match name will be saved in a database which can be queried, counted and manipulated on the website by the teacher and even converted to a download-able excel sheet.
